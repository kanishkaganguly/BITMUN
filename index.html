About BITMUN 2015

For the first time, BITOTSAV '15 is proud to host the BITMUN 2015. With participants from colleges all across India, BITMUN 2015 hopes to set standards for MUNs all across India.

The committees to be featured in this debut MUN include the SOCHUM and the ECOFIN.

The event will take place across three days as part of BITOTSAV '15, from the 20th of March, 2015 to the 22nd of March, 2015.

Further information regarding the agenda and the guidelines can be found below in the Downloads section.
